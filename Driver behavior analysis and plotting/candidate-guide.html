Allstate Phone Screen Candidate Guide

The phone screen is a condensed version of the onsite interview designed to test candidates' technical abilities and experiences. We're also looking for how well candidates will fit into our research culture. Effective communication is a crucial part of our jobs. So we look for candidates that can explain their thinking and their work clearly and concisely. For entry level positions we consider the following nice to haves:

  • Insurance industry experience
  • Data science or machine learning experience
  • Fluency in coding languages or software design principles
  • Knowledge of advanced mathematics or science

Typical candidates have strong backgrounds in at least one of these areas, and potential for growth in the others.

Interview

The interview will begin with introductions and discussion of our mission at Allstate. Please ask questions along the way. We will talk through your resume. You will present the results of you work on the data exercise. See the data-exercise-guide.html for more instructions. We will have you solve some analytical thinking questions aimed at discovering your potential as a scientific coder. It will not be syntax specific. We will make sure you have an opportunity to ask questions at the end of the interview.

Please reach out to your recruiter with any questions.
